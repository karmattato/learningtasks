Chocolate Truffles Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 240 g Condensed Milk
  • 100 g Cocoa Powder

Instructions:

  1. Pour the condensed into a pot and start cooking. Stir till it boils, when it starts bubbling lightly with foams, you should take it off the gas so it doesn't burn.
  2. Sift in the cocoa powder 3 times. Then pour the pure cocoa powder (no sugar) into the milk while it's still hot. Mix.
  3. Sift the second time after the cocoa is almost well-mixed. Keep mixing.
  4. Sift the rest of the cocoa powder in. The mixture should be pretty thick by now.
  5. After mixing all the cocoa, the mixture should harden a bit, but still kinda soft. Put it in a plastic wrap and leave it in the fridge for 2-3 hours before continuing.
  6. After putting the chocolate in the fridge, it will harden and should be easier to cut and shape.
  7. Remember to spread some cocoa powder underneath before cutting it. Cut into even pieces, cut about 15g per piece (size is for preference only).
  8. Sift some cocoa powder on top to prevent sticking while shaping. Normally truffles are shaped as circles because it's quick and easy (shape it however you want).
  9. After shaping, wrap them up nicely or eat it as you're done. If the cocoa powder falls off you can sift some on it again. ENJOY!
