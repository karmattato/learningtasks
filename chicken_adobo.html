Chicken Adobo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kilogram chicken
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon whole black pepper
  • 3 pieces bay leaves

Instructions:

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a pot.
  2. Bring to a boil, then lower the heat and simmer for 30-45 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  3. Serve hot with rice.
